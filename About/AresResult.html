    Ares Results

    After submitting analysis, a new tab appears with an interactive plot of -Log10(adjusted p-value) vs Log2(fold change) of group one - group two. A p-value line shows the 0.05 cutoff, with everything above the line having p-value < 0.05. Some very small p-values result in a value of infinity for -Log10(adjusted p-value) and are marked as a triangle. A table of the top ten data points is displayed bellow the plot and can be sorted by any of the variables.
